Strawberry Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 1 (10 ounce) package frozen sliced strawberries
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ⅔ cup heavy cream

Directions:

Combine the frozen strawberries and sugar in a blender. Process until the fruit is roughly chopped. With the processor running, slowly pour in the heavy cream until fully incorporated. Serve immediately, or freeze for up to one week. (swap strawberries with other frozen fruits!)
