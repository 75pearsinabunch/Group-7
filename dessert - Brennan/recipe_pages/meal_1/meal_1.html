Peanut Butter Bars

Ingredients:

  • 1 Â½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 lb powdered sugar, 4 cups
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 16 ounces creamy peanut butter, 1 1/2 cups
  • 12 ounces milk chocolate chips

Directions:

Line a 9 X 13 baking pan with parchment paper. In a large bowl, combine first four ingredients and mix well with an electric mixer until creamy. Press into the prepared pan. In a microwave safe bowl, melt the chocolate at medium power for 21/2 minutes stirring every 30 seconds until just smooth. Pour over peanut butter mixture and smooth with a spatula. Refrigerate until chocolate topping is firm. Remove bars from the pan using the edges of the parchment paper to lift out of the pan. Cut into bars. Keep refrigerated.
