Chai Tea Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 2 chai tea bags
  • 1/3 cup raw cashews, soaked overnight then drained
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup chia seeds

Directions:

  1. Steep the chai tea bags in the water for 5 minutes, then discard the tea bags.
  2. Add the chai tea, cshews, vanilla extract, maple syrup, cinnamon and nutmeg to a high speed blender and blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour the mixture into a bowl of the chia seeds and whisk togetehr until everything is well incorpoated. Let sit covered in the fridge for one hour or overnight to set.
  4. Spoon the chia pudidng into small cups and top with your favourite yogurt and a sprinkle of cinnmaon
