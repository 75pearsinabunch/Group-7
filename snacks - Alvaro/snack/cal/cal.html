Onion rings with mozzarella cheese

Tips!

Cooking time will vary greatly depending on your temperturem the amount of oil and depth of the pan. Instead of just dropping your food item into the deep fryer, hold it in just below the surface of the oil for about five seconds, then release it with your tongs.

Ingredients:

  • 2 large Onions
  • 3-4 slices of mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups of cake flour
  • 5 eggs
  • Fried oil
  • Marinara sauce

Directions:

1. Cut the onion into 1 cm wide slices and divide into individual rings.

2. Cut the mozzarella cheese into 4 equal parts. Put a small ring in a large ring of onions, put cheese in the gap, and chill in the freezer for 1 hour.

3. Put flour, egg, and bread crumbs in order on (2), and batter the egg and bread crumbs again.

4. Put frying oil in a pan and heat, then fry (3) until golden brown. Be careful not to overheat until the cheese spills.

5. Take it on a paper towel, drain off excess oil, and you're done! Enjoy with marinara sauce!
