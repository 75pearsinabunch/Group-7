Smoked Sausage and Rice Skillet!

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 1 lb Hillshire Farms Smoked Sausage sliced ¼-inch thick
  • 2 cups broccolli florets
  • 1½ cups cooked brown rice - I use minute rice to save time!
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion salt
  • ¼ tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:

Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add butter and olive oil until melted. Add the sliced smoked sausage and cook for about 5 minutes until sausage begins to brown. Add the broccol and cover. Reduce heat to low and cook about 5 minutes more - stirring occasionally. Add the cooked rice, garlic powder and onion salt to the skillet. Stir well. Add the shredded cheese - stiring well until the cheese is melted. Remove from the heat and serve warm.
